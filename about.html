What We Do

WHAT DO WE DO AS COREGAINS

Every month, we source a variety of NEW, trending snacks ranging from big brand suppliers to small startups. Our boxes are packed with: plant-based energy supplements, protein bars, and healthy energizing snacks which are then delivered straight to your door!

Each box will have new snacks and energy supplements that exceed our team’s expectations so it’s a great way to try the best of the best when it comes to healthy snacking!

Why We Do It

WHY WE STARTED WHAT WE DO

We built Core Gains out of a passion to find and distribute the highest quality snacks possible and require all our selections to be: nutrient dense, high in protein, and packed with energy to power you through your day.

Our Story

WHAT MADE IT REAL

Nick Snow, Core Gains CEO, was on the hunt to find the best plant-based snacks for himself to help supplement his busy day and vigorous workout routine. Upon trying a few other snack box services and being dissatisfied, he decided to conduct his own research and personally sampled a plethora of high quality vegan snacks.

After building relationships with his favorite plant-based snack companies and ordering them for himself each month, he created Core Gains Snacks, which now delivers the same high-quality snacks he eats on a daily basis, right to your door!

Nick has a passion for feeling his absolute best each day, maintaining maximum energy, and staying free of all illnesses. He has studied nutrition for the last 15 years and uses that knowledge when selecting whole foods and snacks . His philosophy is that if he does not put it into his own body, he will not sell it.
